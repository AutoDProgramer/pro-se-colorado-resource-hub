Cop Watching Tips

Practical tips and strategies to safely record law enforcement encounters.

Best Practices

  • Remain calm and respectful during interactions.
  • Position yourself at a safe distance when recording.
  • Inform the officer that you are recording if asked, but you are not required to stop unless lawfully directed.

Incident Reporting Guide

Steps for reporting police misconduct:

  • Document details of the incident (date, time, location, officer badge numbers).
  • Submit reports to appropriate authorities using their official complaint forms.
  • Share incident information with watchdog organizations, such as CopWatch.

Video Evidence Guide

Tips for preserving and sharing video evidence:

  • Upload videos to secure cloud storage immediately after recording.
  • Share links with trusted individuals or legal counsel.
  • Consider platforms like Signal for encrypted sharing.
